Famous sport
Soccer and in second place cycling what is the famous sport?
origin Football is the most popular sport in Belgium. The Royal Belgian Football Association (KBVB/URBSFA) is the main governing body of professional football in Belgium and was founded in 1895, although it joined FIFA in 1904 and UEFA in 1954. The KBVB/URBSFA organizes the Belgian First Division —the country's top league competition— and the Belgian Cup, and manages the men's and women's national teams.
how it started?

  • Introduced by the British (1870s–1880s)
    British workers, engineers, traders, and students brought football to Belgium, especially in port cities and industrial areas like Antwerp, Brussels, and Liège.

  • Early clubs (1880s)
    Some of the oldest Belgian clubs were founded by or with British influence:

    • Antwerp FC (founded 1880) – the oldest club still in existence in Belgium

    • FC Liégeois (1892)

    • Club Brugge (1891)

  • Official organization (1895)
    In 1895, Belgium formed its national governing body:

    Union Belge des Sociétés de Sports Athlétiques (UBSSA)
    This later became today’s Royal Belgian Football Association (RBFA).

  • First league competition (1895–96)
    The first Belgian championship was played in the 1895–96 season with just 7 teams.

  • Early international presence
    Belgium played one of the first international matches in football history (against France in 1904) and became a founding member of FIFA the same year
why it grews fast?

  • Compact country → easy travel for matches

  • Strong club culture linked to cities and communities

  • Early international involvement

  • Football became a working-class sport alongside cycling

By the early 20th century, football was already Belgium’s most popular team sport — a position it still holds today.
