In this webpage you will find important information an some data that you may not know about Belgium

Belgium is a small but influential country located in Western Europe. It is known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and strategic position at the heart of Europe. Belgium has three official languages—Dutch, French, and German—which reflect its multicultural society. The country is famous for its medieval cities, delicious chocolate, waffles, and world-class beers. Brussels, the capital city, is also the political center of the European Union, making Belgium an important player in international affairs.
